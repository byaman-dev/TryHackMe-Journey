# 📖 About This Room

Sakura is an investigative Open Source Intelligence (OSINT) room that focuses on collecting, analysing, and verifying publicly available information to answer a series of progressively challenging questions.

Unlike traditional cybersecurity rooms that involve exploiting systems, Sakura emphasises research methodology, evidence correlation, and logical reasoning. Success depends on carefully connecting small pieces of publicly available information rather than relying on technical attacks.

This room reinforced the importance of patience, attention to detail, and validating findings before reaching conclusions.

# 🔍 Investigation Methodology

Rather than searching randomly, I followed a systematic investigation process:

```text
Understand the Question
        │
        ▼
Identify Available Clues
        │
        ▼
Research Public Sources
        │
        ▼
Correlate Information
        │
        ▼
Verify Findings
        │
        ▼
Document the Answer
```

This structured workflow improved both the accuracy and efficiency of the investigation.
