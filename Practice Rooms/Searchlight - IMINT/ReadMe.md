# 📖 About This Room

Searchlight is an introductory Image Intelligence (IMINT) challenge that demonstrates how visual information can be analysed to identify locations, landmarks, and contextual clues from images.

Rather than relying on technical exploitation, this room focuses on observation, critical thinking, and the use of publicly available resources to investigate photographs. It highlights how seemingly insignificant details within an image can reveal valuable intelligence when carefully examined and correlated with external information.

The room develops investigative skills that are widely used in cybersecurity, digital forensics, threat intelligence, journalism, and law enforcement.

# 🌍 Why IMINT Matters

Image Intelligence is an important discipline within Open Source Intelligence (OSINT). Photographs often contain valuable information that can reveal locations, activities, organisations, infrastructure, or events.

Professionals across multiple industries use IMINT to support investigations, verify evidence, and develop situational awareness.

Applications include:

- Threat Intelligence
- Digital Forensics
- Incident Response
- Law Enforcement
- Journalism
- Corporate Investigations
- National Security
- Security Research
